# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Wedding guests, family members, and friends of Lena and Shin. They want to find wedding event details, RSVP, view the schedule/rundown, view couple photos, and easily find registry/digital envelope information.

## Product Purpose
To invite guests to Lena & Shin's wedding on Saturday, April 4th, 2026. The site serves as the central hub for invitations, RSVPs, dynamic wishes, and event schedules.

## Brand Personality
Elegant, Romantic, Luxury (Warm cream, emerald/forest green, and gold tones, classic editorial serif typography, premium smooth animations).

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS product screens, flat dark modes, over-engineered layout animations, poor-contrast text.

## Design Principles
1. **Editorial Elegance**: Treat the screen like a premium printed editorial invite.
2. **Clear Information Architecture**: Key dates, times, and locations must be immediately legible and clear.
3. **Immersive Storytelling**: Background music, love timeline, and photography are prioritized.
4. **Seamless Micro-interactions**: RSVP wishes load instantly, and copy-account buttons work flawlessly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Ensure WCAG AA contrast (≥ 4.5:1 for body copy). Respect prefers-reduced-motion settings by offering static fallback animations.
